ABOUT THE COMPANY

Allied Petrochemical B.V. (Allied) is a private trading company incorporated in 2003 in Rotterdam, the Netherlands  specialized in supply of different commodities to China. Throughout 20 years experience Allied has built up  a solid reputation and stable position among its counterparties and developed strong relationship with major Chinese producers and their trading arms.

Allied moves yearly 1’000’000 MT of different commodities. Majority of revenues fall on rawmaterials for metallurgical industry (various types of coking coal, PCI) and petrochemical goods (LPG,methanol, gas condensate, naphtha).
